Napoleon's Enduring Legacy Shaped Modern Europe

His reforms in law, education, and administration outlasted his empire

Civil law code established 1804, still forms basis of legal systems in 70+ countries worldwide

Educational Reform

Created lycées and grandes écoles system, establishing merit-based education in France

Metric System

Standardized weights and measures across empire, now global standard

Nationalism

Sparked nationalist movements across Europe that reshaped the continent for centuries
